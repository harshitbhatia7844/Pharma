ABOUT US

Founded in 2000, Pharmanet Formulations is a dynamic and forward-thinking pharmaceutical company now headquartered in Greater Noida, Uttar Pradesh, India. From our inception, we have been driven by a commitment to innovation and a passion for advancing healthcare through quality formulations.

Guided by the strategic vision of our Chairperson, Ms. Neha Singh, Pharmanet Formulations, has consistently strived to develop and introduce pharmaceutical products that meet the evolving needs of patients and healthcare providers throughout India. Our growth over the past two decades reflects our dedication to research, development, and the adoption of best practices in pharmaceutical formulation.

At Pharmanet Formulations, we believe that healthcare is a fundamental right, and we are committed to making our products accessible to all. Our extensive portfolio includes a diverse range of formulations, catering to various therapeutic areas, and we continuously invest in research and development to expand our offerings and improve patient outcomes.

As we look to the future, we remain steadfast in our mission to be a leader in the pharmaceutical industry, driven by innovation, quality, and a commitment to improving the health and well-being of individuals across India. Our team of dedicated professionals is passionate about making a positive impact on healthcare, and we are excited about the opportunities that lie ahead as we continue to grow and evolve.

We are more than just a pharmaceutical company; we are a team of dedicated professionals based in Greater Noida, Uttar Pradesh, India, driven by a shared purpose: to improve health outcomes and enhance the quality of life for individuals across the country through our carefully crafted formulations.
